WE ARE ENCORE

Encore was established in early 2024. We are a fully inclusive group of like-minded people who all share a passion for performing in one form or another. Our purpose is to provide a safe and fun environment for our members to perform and share ideas, while supporting each other on our creative journey.

We're always looking for new members and supporters and **welcome all abilities from ages 10+**. Whether you're an actor, singer, dancer, musician, or even a juggler or magician - whatever your talent, you'll find a fun and very warm welcome from everyone at Encore.
