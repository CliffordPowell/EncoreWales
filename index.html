Welcome

Welcome to this evening’s Encore Wales showcase, From Page to Stage. With World Book Day falling this week, it felt like the perfect theme—celebrating the journey of stories from the page to the stage.

Encore Wales has now been established for two years, and it has been an exciting time for us as a group. Last year we were delighted to achieve charitable status, opening up new opportunities for fundraising and helping us grow what we do in the community.

We also staged our biggest production to date, Robin Hood, which received wonderful feedback from audiences and participants alike. It was a real milestone for us and a reminder of what our talented members can achieve together.

Tonight’s performance marks our fifth show and fourth showcase, and we’re delighted to share it with you. Looking ahead, we’re pleased to announce that later this year we’ll be performing School of Rock. Dates and audition details will be announced very soon.

Welcome (cont.)

We should also mention that this evening’s showcase happens to clash with the Wales vs Ireland match in the Six Nations Championship. We’re grateful you’ve chosen to spend the evening with us. And if anyone does happen to hear the score, please keep it to yourself for now—some people may be planning to watch it later and would rather avoid spoilers!

Our appreciation goes to all Encore Wales members who have contributed to tonight’s performance, both on stage and behind the scenes. Their time, enthusiasm and hard work are what make evenings like this possible. We’re also grateful to Faith Church and The Loading Bay for providing refreshments.

Encore Wales is always keen to welcome new members. As our productions grow, we’ll need more helping hands both on stage and backstage—from performers and crew to chaperones and other vital roles.

For now, sit back, relax, and enjoy the show. 🎭
