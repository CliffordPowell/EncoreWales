Febru-Merry with Encore Wales!

February is FREE for taster sessions!

Shake off the January blues and step into the magic of community theatre! Whether you’ve always dreamed of performing, feel a little nervous, or aren’t sure if you have “talent,” Encore Wales welcomes you with open arms.

  • Fun, friendly, and supportive group
  • All ages welcome (10 to 110!)
  • No experience needed
  • Make new friends and build confidence
  • Perform in our next showcase: From Page to Stage (March 2026)

Join us and be part of a group performing with the community, in the community, for the community!
