Why Join Encore Wales!

Encore Wales are always on the hunt for new members

Theatre Kid at heart, and wish you could take to the stage yourself? Not acted since school and fancy giving it a shot again? Or perhaps you more handy and enjoy making costumes or designing sets? Whether you’ve always dreamed of performing, feel a little nervous, or aren’t sure if you have “talent,” Encore Wales welcomes you with open arms.

  • Fun, friendly, and supportive group
  • All ages welcome (10 to 110!)
  • No experience needed
  • Make new friends and build confidence
  • Regular performing opportunities from a group where all abilities are welcome and all voices are heard!
  • Perform in our next showcase, or our end-of-year big show School of Rock (Nov 2026)

Membership Pricing

We’ve kept our membership simple, transparent, and accessible:

  • £20 annual joining fee
  • £20 monthly membership

Our aim is to create a welcoming community that’s affordable for everyone, while still offering a high-quality experience.
If cost is a concern, discreet options are available—please feel free to get in touch for a confidential conversation.

Join us and be part of a group performing with the community, in the community, for the community!
