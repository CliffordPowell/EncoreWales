Welcome to the Band!

Based on the film starring Jack Black and taken to the stage by Lord Andrew Lloyd Webber, Encore Wales will be staging the hit musical School Of Rock later this year.
This West End and Broadway smash hit is a high-octane, heart-warming show that celebrates the power of music. We are looking for a diverse, energetic cast of performers (both kids and adults) who are ready to rock out and give it their all.

Key Dates & Information

  • Audition Date(s): Friday 8th May 7pm - 9pm
  • Location: Faith Church
  • First Rehearsal: Friday 15th May

Preparation

At the audition we will sing through ‘If Only You Would Listen’ and also ‘Time to Play’ all together, and in groups.
You can hear the songs on YouTube, and there will be a link on the Encore WhatsApp group too. Those who wish to be considered for a role which has some vocal solos will be required to sing on their own (a section of one of those two songs), and read a scene (or a few scenes!).
NB: The audition scenes are mostly general ones to cover as many characters as possible, and they may not have every individual named character in.

Principal Roles – Adult

  • Dewey Finn (Guitar): A struggling rock star who poses as a substitute teacher.
    Vocal: Rock-Tenor
  • Rosalie Mullins: The strict headmistress of Horace Green.
    Vocal: Soprano (with rock ‘n’ roll soul)
  • Ned Schneebly: Dewey’s old bandmate, who is now a substitute teacher. Dewey’s best friend.
  • Patty Di Marco: Ned’s overbearing girlfriend who wants Dewey out of the house.

Principal Roles – Child

  • Zack (Guitar): Zack’s skills on the electric guitar are out of this world, but his composition skills might be even better. Zack’s new song in Battle of the Bands proves that this kid is one of a kind—even if his father may be too busy at times to notice.
    Vocal: Tenor
  • Lawrence (Keys): Lawrence plays the keyboard, even though he’s not sure he’s cool enough to be in the band. With a little inspiration from Dewey, Lawrence realizes what’s cool is playing the keys.
  • Katie (Bass): Katie, on bass guitar, uses her years of classical training on the cello to support the band. Who says classical cellists can’t play Zeppelin?
  • Freddie (Drums): Freddie is a straight-laced kid with percussive ambitions, showing that the good kid who follows orders can also march to the beat of their own drum.
    Vocal: Tenor
  • Tomika (Vocals): Tomika is the starlet with the voice, but it’ll take a bit of courage to belt it all out, let alone speak in class. She’s the new kid in school and struggles to find her place—until the band helps her shine.
    Vocal: Mezzo-Soprano
  • Marcy & Shonelle (Backing Vocals / Dance): Two young ladies who can do more than just carry a tune—they can rock it.
  • Summer (Band Manager): Summer is the band’s manager—a role that suits her perfectly. She’s a firecracker, unafraid to tell the others (including Dewey) exactly what to do.
    Vocal: Mezzo-Soprano
  • Billy (Stylist): Billy is the stylist of the group. His interest in fashion is definitely not celebrated at home. Luckily, he’s got an outlet with the band and finds the perfect image for the School of Rock: Horace Prep uniform-chic.
    Vocal: Tenor
  • Mason (Tech): On tech. There’s no doubt that his parents are tightening their hold on his studies. They love to remind him of their sacrifices for his education, but is all of it for him or for them? Mason’s got his own plans.
  • James (Security): Serves as security for the band. He’s going to make sure no one messes with these rock stars in training.
